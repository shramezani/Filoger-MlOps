How CancerAI Works

Our platform uses advanced AI models to process medical imaging data and provide comprehensive analysis. Here's how it works:

  1. Upload Medical Images: Users (doctors or healthcare facilities) upload X-rays, MRIs, or other medical imaging to our secure platform.
  2. AI Analysis: The AI model analyzes the uploaded images, looking for potential abnormalities or early signs of cancer that may be hard to detect with the naked eye.
  3. Report Generation: Within minutes, CancerAI generates a detailed report highlighting potential areas of concern, with a confidence score indicating the likelihood of cancer presence.
  4. Expert Review: Doctors can review the AI-generated report alongside their clinical expertise for a more informed diagnosis and treatment plan.
